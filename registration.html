How to Register

  1. Prepare your accepted paper in IEEE format and ensure final submission before 11 February 2026.
  2. Visit the CMT Registration Portal to complete payment.
  3. Fill in all required details (paper ID, author information, category, and payment details).
  4. Upload the payment receipt/confirmation on the portal for verification.

Please note: Each accepted paper requires at least one registered author to present at the conference.

Important Notes

  • Separate registration is required for multiple papers.
  • Student category requires valid student ID proof.
  • Registration is confirmed only after payment is received.
  • For any queries, contact: tqcebt.lavasa@christuniversity.in
