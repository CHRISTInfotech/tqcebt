About the University

CHRIST (Deemed to be University) was established following the educational vision of St. Kuriakose Elias Chavara, a pioneering educationist and social reformer in 19th-century South India. Since its inception as Christ College in 1969, the institution has grown in academic excellence and infrastructure. It was granted autonomy in 2004 and declared a deemed-to-be University in 2008.

The Pune Lavasa Campus began as Christ Institute of Management in 2014 and achieved Off-Campus status in 2018. Today, it offers UG, PG, and PhD programmes across Commerce, Law, Data Science, Management, and Humanities. Nestled in the Sahyadri hills, the campus provides a serene and digitally advanced environment for learning.

Facilities include smart classrooms, 24/7 high-speed connectivity, modern infrastructure, and a vibrant student life enriched with co-curricular, community service, and research activities.

About the Conference

The 3rd IEEE International Conference on Trends in Quantum Computing & Emerging Business Technologies (TQCEBT 2026) is an interdisciplinary platform bringing together researchers, practitioners, and business leaders. The focus is on exploring the synergy between quantum computing and its applications across science, technology, and business domains.

As Moore’s Law approaches its limits, new computing paradigms like quantum computing are emerging, enabling faster computations, advanced cybersecurity, and innovative business models. Global players including Google, Microsoft, and D-Wave, along with government initiatives like India’s National Mission on Quantum Technologies, highlight the growing importance of this field.

  • First-of-its-kind interdisciplinary conference blending physics, technology, and management.
  • Accepting both technical research and conceptual management frameworks.
  • Tracks spanning AI/ML, FinTech, HealthTech, Quantum Finance, and more.
  • Promotes research and collaboration in the Indian sub-continent and globally.
