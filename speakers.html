Workshop

Kunal Pagariya

Software Technical Expert, Amdocs India Pvt Ltd

Workshop Topic

Building with GenAI and Agentic Systems for Enterprise Automation

Schedule & Venue

April 9, 2026 | 11.30 AM to 1.00 PM and 2.00 PM to 4.00 PM
Auditorium, Central Block, CHRIST University Pune Lavasa Campus

Overview

This workshop will introduce practical approaches to building enterprise workflows with Generative AI and agentic systems. It will cover how intelligent agents can coordinate tasks, reason across business contexts, and support automation at scale. Participants will gain an applied understanding of GenAI-driven orchestration, real-world enterprise use cases, and the design considerations needed for reliable and effective AI-powered systems.
