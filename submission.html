3rd IEEE International Conference on Trends in Quantum Computing & Emerging Business Technologies

Submit via CMT IEEE Conference Templates

Important Dates

  • Full Paper Submission: 23 December 2025
  • Acceptance Notification: 20 January 2026
  • Final Paper Submission: 25 February 2026
  • Conference Dates: 9th - 11th April 2026

Before You Submit

  • Submit original, unpublished work.
  • Ensure compliance with IEEE anti-plagiarism policy.
  • Use the IEEE A4 two-column template; limit to maximum 6 pages including figures and references.
  • Submit and manage your paper via CMT. Edits allowed until the deadline; late submissions will not be accepted.

Conference Tracks (Indicative)

  • Quantum Computing: Quantum Materials, Cryptography, QML, Optimization, Quantum Finance
  • Computing Paradigms: AI/ML/DL, Cloud, Computational Intelligence, Industry 4.0, IoT
  • Emerging Business Technologies: FinTech, EdTech, SpaceTech, HealthTech, AgriTech, Hydro-informatics
  • Applications of AI/ML in Management: Finance, HR, Marketing, Ops, Entrepreneurship
  • Socio-Technical Applications: Economics & Society of EBTs, Smart City/Village, Responsible Innovation, Social Entrepreneurship
  • Ethico-Legal Dimensions: Blockchain, AI, Metaverse, FinTech, Robotics, IPR, Data Protection/Privacy, Cybersecurity, Psychology & AI
